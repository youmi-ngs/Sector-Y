Shaka

Photography SNS for Shutterbugs

"Shooting Journey" - A social app for Shutterbugs. Share your photos, meet other photographers, and find great photo spots on the map.

What You Can Do

  • 📸 Share Photos - Post your photos with location and camera info
  • 📍 Share Location - Show your friends where you are on the map
  • 📱 Lock Screen Widget - See "Shooting Journey" on your lock screen
  • 👥 Make Friends - Follow, like, and comment
  • 🔍 Search - Find photos and people easily
  • 🔔 Get Notifications - Know when someone likes your photo
  • 🔄 Quick Navigation - Tap tab twice to go back to top

Built with

SwiftUI, Firebase, ActivityKit, MapKit, Core Location, Firestore, Cloud Functions
